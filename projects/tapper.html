Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the Tapper mobile application (the "App").

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Any information you enter into the App (such as counter labels or logs) is stored exclusively on your own device's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by the developer or any third parties.

2. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks that track user behavior or collect data.

3. Permissions

The App does not require or request any sensitive permissions (such as Location, Camera, or Contacts) to function.

4. Changes to This Policy

The privacy policy will not be updated time-to-time, as this is only a simple counting app with no data collection. However, it will be good to have a note here if anything changes in the future.
